**Retrieved sections:**

- 3.3 Post-installation Check
- 5.2 Checking LED Indications
- 4.1 Turning the Repeater On or Off

- 3. Installation

**Answer:**

After installing the RD98XS Digital Repeater, you should first confirm that it is powered on. To do this, follow the steps in Section 4.1 Turning the Repeater On or Off.

According to Section 3.3 Post-installation Check, after powering on the repeater, check whether it works properly by observing the nine LED indicators and the LCD display in the front panel.

To understand what each LED indicator means, refer to Section 5.2 Checking LED Indications:

- Power Indicator: Green (normal power-on)
- Alarm Indicator: Red (abnormal operation and alarm pops up)
- Operation Status Indicator:
	+ Green: Repeater is operating in Repeater mode
	+ Off: Repeater is operating in Base mode
- Digital Mode Indicator: Blue (repeater is operating in Digital mode)
- Analog Mode Indicator: Yellow (repeater is operating in Analog mode)
- Timeslot 1 TX Indicator: Red (analog mode: transmitting, digital mode: transmitting in timeslot 1)
- Timeslot 1 RX Indicator: Green (analog mode: receiving, digital mode: receiving in timeslot 1)
- Timeslot 2 TX Indicator: Red (digital mode: transmitting in timeslot 2)
- Timeslot 2 RX Indicator: Green (digital mode: receiving in timeslot 2)

Note that the power level is indicated on the LCD display as per Section 4.3 Adjusting the Power Level, with "High" and "Low" power levels displayed accordingly.
