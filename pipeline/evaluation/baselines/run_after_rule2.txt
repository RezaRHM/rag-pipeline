7. Timeslot 1 RX Indicator: Green (analog mode: receiving, digital mode: receiving in timeslot 1)
8. Timeslot 2 TX Indicator: Red (digital mode: transmitting in timeslot 2)
9. Timeslot 2 RX Indicator: Green (digital mode: receiving in timeslot 2)
