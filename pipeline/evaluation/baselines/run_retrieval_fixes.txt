The RD98XS Digital Repeater has nine LED indicators that can be checked after installation and powering on the repeater, as described in Section 3.3 Post-installation Check of the documentation. The LED indications are listed in detail in Section 5.2 Checking LED Indications.

Here is a summary of the LEDs:

1. Power Indicator: Green (normal power-on)
2. Alarm Indicator: Red (abnormal operation and alarm pops up)
3. Operation Status Indicator:
	* Green (repeater operating in Repeater mode)
	* Off (repeater operating in Base mode)
4. Digital Mode Indicator: Blue (repeater operating in Digital mode)
5. Analog Mode Indicator: Yellow (repeater operating in Analog mode)
6. Timeslot 1 TX Indicator:
	* Red (analog mode: transmitting, digital mode: transmitting in timeslot 1)
7. Timeslot 1 RX Indicator:
	* Green (analog mode: receiving, digital mode: receiving in timeslot 1)
8. Timeslot 2 TX Indicator: Red (digital mode: transmitting in timeslot 2)
9. Timeslot 2 RX Indicator: Green (digital mode: receiving in timeslot 2)

These LEDs provide visual feedback on the status of the repeater, allowing for easy monitoring and troubleshooting.

Q7: What does each front-panel LED state mean on the HR652?
======================================================================
[type=standard]
According to the provided context, here are the meanings of each front-panel LED on the HR652 Digital Repeater:

1. **Digital mode indicator**: Glows blue when the repeater is in digital mode.
2. **Analog mode indicator**: Glows orange when the repeater is in analog mode.
3. **Alarm indicator**: Glows red and displays a corresponding alarm code (E1, E2, or EH/EH for under/over-voltage) when an exception occurs.
4. **Timeslot A TX indicator**: Glows red when transmitting in analog mode or Timeslot A is transmitting in digital mode.
5. **Timeslot A RX indicator**: Glows green when receiving in analog mode or Timeslot A is receiving in digital mode.
6. **Timeslot B TX indicator**: Glows red when Timeslot B is transmitting in digital mode.
7. **Timeslot B RX indicator**: Glows green when Timeslot B is receiving in digital mode.

Note that the seven-segment LED displays additional information, such as alarm codes (E1, E2, EH/EH) or channel numbers, depending on the situation.
